What is Astrology?

Astrology is an ancient science that studies the movement and position of planets, stars, and celestial bodies to understand their influence on human life. By analyzing your date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth, astrology provides deep insights into your personality, career, relationships, health, and future.

How Does Astrology Work?

Astrology works by creating a birth chart (kundali) based on the exact position of planets at the time of your birth. Each planet represents specific energies, and their interaction reveals opportunities, challenges, and life patterns.

A professional astrologer studies these planetary combinations to provide accurate guidance, remedies, and predictions tailored specifically for you.
